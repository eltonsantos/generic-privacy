# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications, browser extensions, and
digital products developed and published by Elton Santos.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our products are designed
to operate with minimal data collection and maximum transparency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🔍 Data Collection

By default, our applications and extensions do not collect, store, or
share personal data, unless explicitly stated within the specific
product.

We do NOT collect:

-   ❌ Name, email address, or contact information (unless required for
    authentication features)
-   ❌ Browsing history
-   ❌ Device identifiers (for tracking purposes)
-   ❌ Location data
-   ❌ Third-party cookies
-   ❌ Analytics or telemetry data (unless explicitly disclosed)
-   ❌ Biometric or sensitive personal information

If a product requires authentication (e.g., Google login), only the
minimum necessary data is accessed and processed securely via the
provider's official SDK.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💾 Local Data Storage

Some applications or extensions may use:

-   Browser Storage APIs (e.g., Chrome Storage)
-   Local device storage
-   Secure local databases

This storage is used exclusively to:

-   Save user preferences
-   Store configuration settings
-   Maintain locally selected items (e.g., tickers, lists, filters)

All such data remains stored locally on the user's device and is not
transmitted to external servers unless required for core functionality.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🌐 External Communication

Certain applications may communicate with external APIs strictly to
provide their primary functionality (for example, retrieving financial
market data).

These requests:

-   Contain no personal user data
-   Transmit only functional parameters (e.g., asset tickers, query
    terms)
-   Are performed over secure HTTPS connections

We do not control third-party API privacy policies, and users should
review them independently when applicable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💳 Subscriptions and Payments

If a product offers premium features via subscription or one-time
purchase:

-   Payments are processed exclusively by official platform providers
    (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store, Stripe, ExtensionPay, etc.)
-   We do not collect or store credit card information
-   Purchase validation is handled via secure platform mechanisms

Subscription status is linked to the user's platform account
(Google/Apple/Browser account), not to personal data stored by us.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📢 Advertising (If Applicable)

Some applications may display advertisements provided by third-party ad
networks.

In such cases:

-   Ads are managed by the platform provider
-   We do not access personal advertising identifiers
-   We do not sell or share user data

If an application offers a paid version, ads may be removed upon
successful purchase verification.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🔐 Permissions Usage

Applications and extensions may request permissions strictly necessary
for functionality.

Examples:

  Permission       Purpose
  ---------------- ------------------------------------------
  Storage          Save user preferences locally
  Network access   Fetch remote API data
  Notifications    Alert users when configured events occur
  Authentication   Enable secure login when available

No permission is used for tracking or surveillance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 👤 Third-Party Services

Our products may integrate:

-   Authentication providers (Google Sign-In, etc.)
-   Payment processors
-   Financial data APIs
-   Cloud infrastructure providers

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data.
