Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications, browser extensions, and digital products developed and published by Elton Santos.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our products are designed to operate with minimal data collection and maximum transparency.

Data Collection

By default, our applications and extensions do not collect, store, or share personal data unless explicitly stated.

  • No name, email, or contact information (unless required for authentication)
  • No browsing history tracking
  • No device tracking identifiers
  • No location data
  • No analytics or telemetry (unless disclosed)
  • No biometric or sensitive personal data

Local Data Storage

Some products may use local storage strictly to:

  • Save user preferences
  • Store configuration settings
  • Maintain locally selected items

This data remains stored only on the user's device.

External Communication

Applications may connect to external APIs solely to provide core functionality (e.g., retrieving financial market data).

These requests do not include personal data and are transmitted securely via HTTPS.

Subscriptions and Payments

If premium features are offered, payments are processed exclusively through official platform providers (Google Play, Apple App Store, Stripe, ExtensionPay, etc.).

We do not store credit card information.

Advertising (If Applicable)

Some applications may display ads provided by third-party ad networks. We do not sell or share user data.

Permissions Usage

Permission Purpose

Network Access Fetch remote API data
Notifications Alert users when configured events occur
Authentication Enable secure login when available

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by modifying the “Last updated” date above.

Children’s Privacy

Our products are not directed toward children under 13 (or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction).
